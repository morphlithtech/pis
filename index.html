Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

Enpis ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application developed for the Enugu Geographic Information System – Property Information System (ENGIS-PIS). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Enpis mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you use the App, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Account Credentials: Email address and password (used for authentication).
  • Identity Information: Title, first name, middle name, last name, National Identification Number (NIN), and NIN verification status.
  • Contact Information: Up to two phone numbers.
  • Employment Information: Employment type, employment ID, designation, and assigned role.
  • Profile Image: Photographs you upload as your profile picture.

b. Location Data

With your permission, we collect your device's precise (GPS) location data, including latitude and longitude coordinates, to:

  • Display your current position on the map.
  • Support field data collection, property search, bill distribution, and enforcement activities.
  • Provide map-based navigation and street view features.

Location data is collected only while the App is in use (foreground permission).

c. Camera and Microphone

With your permission, we access your device's camera and microphone to:

  • Capture photographs for field data collection, enforcement, and property documentation.
  • Record audio when required for enforcement or field activities.

d. Device Information

We automatically collect basic device information, including:

  • Device manufacturer and model name.
  • Operating system and platform type.

e. Push Notification Tokens

If you grant notification permissions, we collect your Expo Push Notification token to deliver in-app and push notifications related to tasks, updates, and alerts.

f. Locally Stored Data

Certain data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Authentication tokens (stored securely via encrypted local storage).
  • Clock-in/clock-out attendance records.
  • User preferences (e.g., fingerprint/biometric authentication settings).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate and authorize your access to the App.
  • Facilitate property management operations, including property search, bill distribution, enforcement, field data collection, and change requests.
  • Display maps and geospatial data relevant to your assigned zones and tasks.
  • Track attendance via the clock-in/clock-out feature.
  • Send push notifications about task assignments, updates, and system alerts.
  • Improve the App's performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Ensure security by verifying identity and managing session tokens.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Government Authorities: As the App is operated for the Enugu State Government's property information system, your data may be shared with relevant government agencies in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Backend Service Providers: Your data is transmitted to our secure backend API for processing and storage.
  • Third-Party Map Services: We use Google Maps and Mapbox to provide mapping functionality. Your location data may be processed by these services in accordance with their respective privacy policies:
    • Google Maps Privacy Policy
    • Mapbox Privacy Policy
  • Expo/EAS Services: Push notification tokens are sent to Expo's push notification service to deliver notifications.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using encrypted local storage.
  • Data in transit is protected using HTTPS/TLS encryption when communicating with our backend servers.
  • Server-side data is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Your personal information is retained for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the services.
  • Locally stored data (attendance records, preferences) persists on your device until you clear the App data or uninstall the App.
  • Upon account termination or deactivation, your data may be retained as required by applicable laws and regulations.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Delete your personal data, subject to legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for location, camera, microphone, or notification permissions at any time through your device settings.
  • Request data portability of your personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps Platform – for map rendering and geocoding
  • Mapbox – for advanced map visualization
  • Expo Application Services (EAS) – for app updates and push notifications
